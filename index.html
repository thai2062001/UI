About me

Hello, my name is Phạm Thị Hải Yến, and I am a marketing professional with a focus on content creation and advertising. After earning my degree in Business Administration in 2018, I pursued further studies in Japan to deepen my expertise in the service industry. Although the pandemic disrupted my plans for hands-on internships, it gave me an unexpected opportunity to explore my passion for writing through blogging, which ultimately led me to a career in content creation.

02
Returning to Vietnam

Upon returning to Vietnam in 2022, I faced a pivotal decision: to become a translator for a Japanese company or pursue my passion for content. Choosing content, I joined a Vietnamese company where I honed my skills in writing for websites, social media, video scripts, and even producing internal podcasts. This experience provided me with a solid foundation in marketing.

03
Transition to Advertising

In 2023, eager to expand my expertise, I transitioned into advertising with GMO Internet Group (Nikko Vietnam), a leading Japanese technology firm. There, I managed advertising campaigns for major clients such as Ajinomoto, Don Quijote, and Airtrip, gaining valuable experience in performance marketing and automation tools that enhanced my productivity.

04
Future Aspirations

With three years of experience in the marketing industry, I am driven by the desire to explore diverse, international markets and grow into a marketing planner. I am confident that my passion, adaptability, and hands-on experience will make me a valuable asset to any organization I join.

About me

Hello! I'm Pham Thi Hai Yen, a passionate and dedicated professional with a keen interest in architecture and design. With a background that blends creativity and technical skills, I thrive on bringing innovative concepts to life. I believe that architecture is not just about buildings; it’s about creating spaces that enhance the quality of life for people. My journey began in Barranquilla, Colombia, a city steeped in history and culture. Inspired by its vibrant streets and rich architectural heritage, I developed a deep appreciation for the interplay between design and community. Through my work, I aim to capture the essence of the environments I design, creating spaces that are both functional and beautiful.
